Our Drive

Many large corporations don't really care about how much financial and economic damage  they cause when throwing tons of food in the trash. Out of the 58% of wasted food, 32% of  it could have been consumed, in a study conducted in 2019 more than 4 million people in  Canada struggle to get enough to eat. The intention of such an application is to encourage  food corporations to give any unwanted produce to those in need instead of throwing  them out. ​One Heart (Non-governmental organization) ​aims to help solve this problem  by assisting facilities like homeless-shelters and low-income centers to access this website  and request any available unwanted produce that the corporations have posted on that  site. The website will have 2 primary users (Corporations, and Homeless-shelters) and 1  Secondary user( Admins) that will be responsible for recovering any lost accounts, and add  new corporations/review any corporations/assistant facilities that would like to contribute  in helping (providing or giving goods) around our country. The corporations that donate will  receive “one heart” icons to show their contribution to helping others.
