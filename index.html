One Good Deed Is All The Heart Needs

We link homeless shelters, low-income centers, and food banks with food companies to give to those in need.

Audience

  1. Food Corporations(fast food ,grocery stores) 
  2. Homeless-shelters and Low-income centers.

Our Goal

1​. Link homeless shelters, low-income centers, and food banks with big companies that  throw away unexpired produce, to be consumed by those in need. 
2. Corporations that contribute to helping those in need, are rewarded with one heart  icon that displays their commitment to giving back.
3. Eventually make it mandatory for such action as part of the law in Canada, just like  what’s implemented in South Korea, Denmark, Norway, France, UEA, and Australia. 
4. To make Canada one of those countries that can contribute to stopping throwing  away foods when they can be consumed by someone else or be able to donate it  when possible​.
